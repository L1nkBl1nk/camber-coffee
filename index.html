Our story

We started with one machine and an idea — never to rush.

Back in 2018, Mia and Theo rented a corner of an old building on Gertrude Street in Fitzroy. They set up one machine, a timber shelf and a single stool for their first guests. Almost everything has changed since — except the way we treat the craft.

We roast our beans in-house, in a small workshop just behind the wall. Our milk comes from a family farm in the Yarra Valley. And we remember everyone who walks in for their morning cup.
